Valorant

Valorant is a highly popular, free-to-play, first-person tactical shooter developed and published by Riot Games. Released in 2020, it has quickly gained a massive following due to its unique blend of precise gunplay and character-based abilities. In Valorant, players are divided into two teams of five, attacking or defending, and they compete in a best-of-25 format. Each player selects an "Agent" with unique skills, adding a strategic layer to the gameplay beyond traditional shooter mechanics. The game emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and clutch plays, making it both challenging and rewarding for players of all skill level
